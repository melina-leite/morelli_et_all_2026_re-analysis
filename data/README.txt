Dataset origin:

Morelli, Federico; Benedetti, Yanina; Mikula, Peter; Díaz, Mario; Tryjanowski, Piotr (2025). Urban birds FID and observers sex. figshare. Dataset. https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.28284848.v1

The phylogenetic bird tree was shared by the corresponding author, Federico Morelli, via email correspondence with Melina Leite on June 15, 2026.


The Rdata data.s_clean.Rdata is the processed data of both, the phylogenetic tree (the covariance matrix) and the experiment (data.s). The data were processed in the main script of the analysis (morelli_et_all_2026_re-analysis.qmd.
